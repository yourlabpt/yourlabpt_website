## Mission

Turn business ideas into working products. YourLab exists to close the gap between "I have an idea" and "this is real." We build MVPs — deliberately small, deliberately fast — so founders and businesses can prove what works before they commit fully.

We don't sell hours. We sell clarity: we help people understand exactly what to build, and then we build it.

## Vision

A world where no good idea dies because the person who had it didn't know how to build it. We believe a focused specialist with a clear scope can move faster and smarter than a big team with a big budget.

The cost of building a custom solution has never been lower. The barrier that used to separate "people with ideas" from "people who can ship products" is gone. YourLab is the bridge.

## Philosophy

**"Start small. Prove it. Scale what's real."**

We are skeptical of big plans and large upfront investments. Every feature that ships before the problem is validated is waste. Every stakeholder meeting that replaces a real user conversation is a mistake. We build the smallest meaningful thing, put it in front of real people, and let reality decide what comes next.

This isn't minimalism for its own sake. It's respect for the founder's money, time, and energy.

## How We Work (The YourLab Process)

### 1. Discover
We start by understanding the problem, not the solution. We ask uncomfortable questions. We push back on assumptions. We try to understand who is actually suffering from this problem and why the existing options aren't working for them.

### 2. Scope
We define what the MVP needs to prove — and nothing more. We write a requirements document that describes behaviour, not technology. This is where most projects save the most money: by saying "not yet" to the right features.

### 3. Build
One specialist per project. Full ownership, full accountability. No hand-offs, no committees, no diffusion of responsibility. The person who scoped it builds it.

### 4. Launch
We deploy to real users as fast as possible. A product nobody has used is not a product — it's a hypothesis. Launch is not the end; it's when the real learning begins.

### 5. Measure & Iterate
We define success metrics before we build, so we know what the data is telling us afterwards. Then we decide together: kill it, pivot, or scale what's working.

## Culture & Values

**Lean thinking.** We cut what isn't essential. Complexity is the enemy of good decisions. Every feature, every meeting, every slide exists for a reason or it doesn't exist.

**Radical honesty.** We tell clients when an idea needs rethinking — not just when they want to hear yes. The kindest thing we can do is say "this scope won't prove what you think it will" before the money is spent.

**Craft.** We take the work seriously. Good code, good architecture, good requirements. We are proud of what we ship, not just that we shipped.

**Speed with intent.** Fast doesn't mean sloppy. It means focused. We are fast because we are clear, not because we cut corners.

**Full ownership.** No one passes the problem to someone else. If you're on a project, you're responsible for it start to finish.

**Respect for the founder.** Every person who hires YourLab is taking a bet. We honour that by treating every project like it's our own company.

## Frequent Questions & Honest Answers

**"I just have an idea, I haven't validated it yet — is it too early to talk to you?"**
No. That's the right time. We help people figure out what to build before we build it.

**"Do I need a technical background?"**
None at all. We are used to translating between the business problem and the technical solution.

**"How long does an MVP take?"**
Depends on the scope, but a well-scoped MVP typically takes 4–10 weeks, not months.

**"What if I want to keep building after the MVP?"**
We can continue with the next scope phase, or we can hand off clean code and documentation to a team you bring in. We don't lock clients in.

**"What's the difference between a scope from YourLab and a quote from a dev agency?"**
A quote from an agency tells you how much a solution costs. A scope from YourLab tells you whether you're building the right solution first. Then we build it.
